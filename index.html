001 — THE MOVEMENT

What started in the gullies of Mumbai and the basements of Delhi is now the loudest youth culture on the subcontinent — five hundred million streams at a time.

Hindi. Punjabi. Urdu. Tamil. Malayalam. Marathi. Haryanvi. The language changes every hundred kilometres — the drums don't. DHH catalogues the records that built the culture: from Bayaan to Big Dawgs, Kohinoor to Lunch Break.

This is not a playlist. It's a planetarium. Every cover you saw in the sphere is a real release that moved the culture forward — mapped, tagged and orbiting forever.
